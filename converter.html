HOW IT WORKS

Converts HTML + CSS into Webflow elements. Click convert, then paste (Cmd/Ctrl+V) on the Webflow canvas. Full HTML documents are accepted — DOCTYPE/html/head are skipped, <body> becomes the wrapper div. JS is embedded as a Code Embed, :hover becomes a Webflow hover variant.
